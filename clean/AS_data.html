    Welcome to the American Samoa Government Website.  This website fulfills one of our objectives to be transparent by providing information on the work the American Samoa Government is doing for the people of American Samoa.  It is important that the public is informed on how their funds have been spent and what public good was generated from these public expenditures.

    ​

    It is our fervent hope that you find this website informative and comprehensive in its attempt to provide factual information on the activities and accomplishments of the American Samoa Government.  Continual website improvement is dependent on your feedback and recommendations on how to enhance its value to the users.
