CoronaVirus (COVID-19) Updates

Latest: Updated Case Numbers; New Restaurant, Bar Rules


State officials will announce new guidelines for social distancing and requirement for Utah restaurants at 4:30 p.m. You can stream the news conference live.

Food service prohibitions: Salt Lake County is prohibiting dine-in food service at restaurants and bars, effective at 11 p.m. Monday. Summit County issued a similar prohibition Sunday. Take-out, delivery and grocery shopping is still permitted but with tighter rules.

Current cases: A total of 29 Utah residents and seven visitors have tested positive for the virus. That includes the first case in Wasatch County, where a student who attends Wasatch High School tested positive

Diamond Princess: Sunday evening, 23 passengers from the Diamond Princess cruise ship that docked in Oakland last week retuned

Slopes Shut Down: Most Utah ski resorts

Schools, churches canceled: Beginning today, Utah's public schools will cancel

Many local governments have closed public buildings, including recreation centers, libraries, and senior centers.

The Utah System of Higher Education, meanwhile, is canceling

State officials are encouraging everyone to take serious precautions, especially staying at least six-feet away from other people and staying home
