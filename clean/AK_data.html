How is DHSS responding to COVID-19?

The Alaska Department of Health and Social Services is actively responding to the COVID-19 pandemic in Alaska. We are working with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and other federal, Tribal, state and local partners to respond to this continuously evolving public health threat. We provide information about how to keep yourself and your family healthy on this website, but you can learn more about Alaska's other response efforts by visiting:

  • COVID-19 News and Video from the Office of Governor Mike Dunleavy
  • COVID-19 Economic Stabilization
