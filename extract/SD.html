BACK TO TOP WHAT TO DO IF YOU DEVELOP SYMPTOMS If you develop symptoms: Call your health care provider immediately and tell them about any recent travel or exposure. Individuals who are concerned that they have COVID-19 should contact their healthcare provider via phone before going to a clinic or hospital to prevent spread in healthcare facilities.
