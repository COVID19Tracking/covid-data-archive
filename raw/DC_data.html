Report Price Gouging

Price gouging is any immediate markup on goods and anything more than a 10 percent increase on services from the 90-day average. It’s illegal. If you feel you’ve been a victim of price gouging, DC Government has a rapid response team to investigate during this state of emergency for coronavirus (COVID-19).
