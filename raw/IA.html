Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19)

There is an expanding global outbreak of a respiratory illness caused by a novel (new) coronavirus. The disease the virus causes has been named COVID-19. While most COVID-19 cases are associated with travel, person-to-person spread is now being reported in other places, including Iowa.
